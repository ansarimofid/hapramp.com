Hapramp Studio is an Idea Lab.

We work on new technologies to make new things for the people around us. Most of the ideas that we are working on sounds a little sci-fi, difficult, yet inspiring. This page will be the repository of everything new and uncooked happening at the Hapramp Studio.

Things we are working on

1Ramp

1Ramp is a social earning platform for creators and artists. We always felt the need of an easier way for creators to showcase their profile, monetize their content, and collaborate with the online community. 1Ramp is building one platform for all those who create on the Internet, and while they are at it, we’ll make sure that they earn money and control how their data is being used.

Content Monetisation

How often do you say that “I found this on the Internet”? What we call ‘The Internet’ today is built by billions of people. People made the Internet into a giant network full of knowledge, entertainment, and possibilities. We got that part right, but we are still figuring out how to monetise people’s contribution the right way. We are exploring and building ways to help people earn money by adding adding value to the Internet.

Data Privacy

We treat data like a physical property. Today, humans create and upload X GB of data on the social media everyday, we spend X hours on an average on our mobile phones everyday, without realising that we are interacting with an ever expanding directory of real human data. We are building technological solutions to give people control over their data and own it like a physical property.
